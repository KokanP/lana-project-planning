1. Project Overview

This document outlines the plan and estimated costs for the Lana Project, focusing on the re-launch and continued development of LANA Coin. The project involves several phases, from team formation and planning to development, marketing, community building, launch, and post-launch activities. The goal is to update the project's infrastructure, engage the community, and establish LANA Coin on relevant platforms.

Disclaimer: This plan is a general outline and requires input from project owners. The cost estimations are based on specific assumptions detailed in the budget section. The timeline is currently To Be Determined (TBD).

2. Project Scope & Objectives

  • Update and potentially redevelop the LANA Coin blockchain and associated wallets.
  • Develop and execute a revised "coin sale" or promotional equivalent strategy.
  • Plan and execute token distribution.
  • Build and engage a community through updated communication channels (e.g., Discord/Slack), social media, content creation, events, and potentially airdrops and an ambassador program.
  • Establish partnerships with exchanges, vendors, and potentially influencers.
  • Ensure legal compliance.
  • Successfully re-launch the mainnet.
  • Secure listings on major cryptocurrency exchanges.
  • Provide user onboarding support.
  • Engage in continuous development, marketing, and community engagement post-launch.

4. Project Plan, Tasks & Estimated Costs

This section combines the project phases with task-specific cost estimations, incorporating a risk/reward model for LANA token payments. Costs are shown in Euros (€) and optionally in LANA tokens based on the assumptions below.

Important Assumptions & Disclaimers (for values shown in tables):

  • SAMPLE Values: Tasks, time estimates, rates, and reward parameters are illustrative placeholders.
  • Static Data: Values in the cost tables are static examples calculated using the specific assumptions listed here.
  • Time Estimates: 'Rough Est. Hours' are speculative guesses.
  • EUR Rate Adjustment (%): Assumed to be 10% for the calculations below. This means the effective hourly rate used for EUR cost calculation is 0.1 times the 'Base Est. Hourly Rate (€)' listed. This models a scenario where contributors accept a lower effective EUR rate for higher potential LANA compensation.
  • Cost Calculation (€): 'Calculated Task Cost (€)' = (Base Est. Hourly Rate (€) * (EUR Rate Adjustment % / 100)) * Rough Est. Hours. For the tables below: (Base Rate * 0.1) * Hours.
  • Currency: All monetary values are in Euros (€).
  • LANA Payment Option & Risk/Reward: An alternative payment method uses LANA tokens. The estimated LANA cost reflects payment adjusted by a multiplier relative to the specified market rate (assumed 1 LANA = 0.0011 EUR below) to account for team risk/reward.
  • LANA Payment Multiplier: Assumed to be 10x for the calculations below. This multiplier increases the amount of LANA paid relative to the calculated EUR cost, compensating contributors for token volatility risk with higher potential upside.
  • Cost Calculation (LANA): Est. Task Cost (LANA) = (Calculated Task Cost (€) * Multiplier) / Current LANA/EUR Rate. For the tables below: (Calculated € Cost * 10) / 0.0011.
